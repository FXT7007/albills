Frequently asked questions

Does it handle invoices from different vendors?
Yes. Albills uses AI to understand invoice layouts, so it works across thousands of templates — from Amazon and Uber to custom supplier invoices.
Can it extract line items, not just totals?
Yes. Each line item — description, quantity, unit price, tax, line total — is extracted into its own row in Excel.
Is it accurate enough for accounting?
We benchmark at 99% field accuracy on clean PDFs and 95%+ on scans. Every field can be reviewed and corrected before export.
Can I export directly to QuickBooks or Xero?
Yes — the Business plan includes one-click export to QuickBooks Online and Xero. Other tools (Zoho Books, FreshBooks) via Zapier.
What about multi-currency invoices?
Currency is detected per invoice and preserved. For totals across currencies, we can apply a reference rate from the invoice date.
