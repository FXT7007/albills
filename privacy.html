Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026. This policy explains what we collect, what we don’t, and your rights as a user of AlBills.

The short version: We never store your invoices. They’re sent to our AI for reading and deleted immediately. We collect only what we need to run your account.

1. What we collect

  • Account data — name, email, hashed password (or auth provider ID).
  • Usage data — credits remaining, extraction count, processing logs (without document content).
  • Payment data — handled entirely by our payment processor (Gumroad). We never see card numbers.
  • Cookies — only what is required for authentication and session state.

2. What we don’t store

  • The contents of any invoice, PDF, image or document you upload.
  • Files are streamed to our AI provider (Anthropic / Claude), processed, and discarded.
  • We do not use your documents to train models.

3. Third parties

  • Supabase — account database and authentication.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI processing of uploaded documents.
  • Gumroad — payment processing for credit purchases.
  • Vercel — hosting.

4. Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and any associated metadata at any time by emailing intothestillnes@gmail.com.

5. Contact

For any privacy questions: intothestillnes@gmail.com
